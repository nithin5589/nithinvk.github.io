Hi, I'm Nithin Veerendranath K.

I am a Mechanical Engineer with PhD-level research experience focused on translating computational mechanics, CAD design, and simulation into industry-ready engineering solutions. My work spans nonlinear FEA, material modeling, scientific computing, and rapid prototyping to support data-driven mechanical design decisions.

What I work on

Industry-focused expertise

Bridging simulation, design, and prototyping to engineer reliable mechanical systems.

Computational Mechanics & FEA

Nonlinear finite element analysis and large-deformation modeling for component- and system-level problems. Experience with custom material models, parametric studies, and performance validation using Abaqus, FEniCS, Python, and ParaView.

Polymers, Degradation & Multiphysics

Physics-based modeling of swelling, degradation, crystallinity evolution, and transport-driven deformation to predict long-term reliability and failure mechanisms in polymeric components.

CAD Design & Rapid Prototyping

Parametric 3D CAD and tolerance-aware mechanical design integrated with simulation workflows. Hands-on experience with rapid prototyping and additive manufacturing (FDM, SLA) to fabricate, test, and iterate components, closing the loop between computation and physical validation.

Selected projects

Simulation- and design-driven projects emphasizing engineering impact.

Biodegradable Heart Stent

3D CAD and nonlinear FEA of a polymeric heart stent evaluated under a simulated 3-month physiological loading cycle. Results guided geometry refinement and material selection to improve durability while preserving deployability.

Swelling-Driven Deformation Modeling

Coupled transport–mechanics framework implemented in FEniCS to predict time-dependent deformation, stress evolution, and tolerance changes relevant to component lifetime.

Nonlinear Constitutive Modeling

Development and calibration of nonlinear constitutive models capturing large-strain behavior and environmental effects to support simulation-driven design decisions.

Simulation Automation

Python-based automation for preprocessing, batch post-processing, and visualization, significantly reducing analysis turnaround time and enabling scalable design studies.
